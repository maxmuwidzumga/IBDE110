Annuals

For the last 15 years we have developed a great selection of annuals that we grow at our local greenhouse. We are now a major supplier of annuals throughout British Columbia and Alberta, and you will be able to find our plants at local department stores such as Wal-Mart and Safeway.

Some of the latest varieties of favourite annuals being grown at our greenhouse are:

  • Petunias (10 different colours)
  • Impatience (5 different colours)
  • Daisy (5 different cultivars)
  • Begonias (3 different cultivars in 4 different colours)

Minimum orders for these annuals are 1000 packs of any container size. Container sizes range from 0.3 to 1 litre. Orders must be placed in the fall before the end of November, and your orders will be shipped to your store as early as the following March.
